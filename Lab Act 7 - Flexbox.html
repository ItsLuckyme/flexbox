Jungle Cruise

"Jungle Cruise" is a 2021 action-adventure film based on the Disney theme park attraction of the same name. Starring Dwayne Johnson and Emily Blunt, the movie follows the adventurous journey of Dr. Lily Houghton and skipper Frank Wolff down the Amazon River in search of a mythical healing tree. The film is filled with humor, action, and CGI effects, making it an entertaining watch for both families and fans of adventure movies like Indiana Jones and Jumanji.

Avatar

"Avatar: The Way of Water" is the highly anticipated sequel to James Cameron's 2009 blockbuster "Avatar." The film is expected to continue the story of the Na'vi and their world, Pandora, as well as explore new themes and technological advancements. Keep an eye out for updates as the release date approaches, and get ready to dive back into the visually stunning and immersive world of "Avatar."

Treasure Planet

"Treasure Planet" is a captivating 2002 animated film that reimagines Robert Louis Stevenson's classic "Treasure Island" in a futuristic setting. Directed by John Musker and Ron Clements, the movie takes viewers on a thrilling journey across the universe as young Jim Hawkins and a cosmic crew, led by the daring Captain Amelia, embark on an exhilarating treasure hunt. Aboard a glittering space galleon, Jim encounters the ship's cyborg cook, John Silver, who imparts valuable lessons on friendship and the power of dreams.

Luca

"Luca" is a heartwarming 2021 animated film set in a beautiful seaside town on the Italian Riviera. Directed by Enrico Casarosa, the movie follows the unforgettable summer adventures of Luca, a bright and inventive 13-year-old sea monster, and his newfound best friend, Alberto. The two friends share gelato, pasta, and endless scooter rides, but their fun is threatened by a deeply-held secret: they are sea monsters from another world just below the waters' surface. As they navigate the human world, they form a deep bond and embark on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and acceptance.
